What We Do

Our Services

09 specialisations
🔗

01

Chain Link Fencing

Galvanised steel chain link fencing built to withstand Nigeria's climate. Suitable for residential boundaries, schools, sports facilities, and commercial perimeters. Strong, low-maintenance, and available in various heights and gauges to suit your security requirement.

⚡

02

Electric Fencing

High-voltage electric perimeter fencing designed to deter, detect, and alert. Ideal for residential estates, corporate facilities, warehouses, and government installations. Each system includes an energiser unit, warning signage, and optional alarm integration for maximum security.

🪡

03

Barbed Wire Systems

Single-strand, double-strand, and concertina (razor wire) barbed wire systems installed along wall tops, fences, and perimeter boundaries. An effective and affordable deterrent for residential, commercial, and industrial properties requiring an added layer of security.

🏭

04

Industrial Perimeter

Heavy-duty perimeter fencing solutions engineered for factories, logistics depots, oil and gas facilities, and large-scale industrial compounds. We manage high linear-metre projects across varied terrain, delivering on time and to specification with minimal disruption to site operations.

🏡

05

Estate & Housing

Perimeter and boundary fencing for residential estate developments, gated communities, and private compounds. We work directly with developers, contractors, and project managers from ground-break through to handover, ensuring boundaries are secured at every stage of construction.

🚪

06

Gates & Access Points

Design, supply, and installation of manual and motorised gates including sliding gates, swing gates, and pedestrian access points. Fabricated in steel to complement your fencing system and engineered for durability, smooth operation, and long-term reliability.

🏟️

07

Playground Fencing

Purpose-built fencing for school perimeters, recreational parks, football pitches, and sports courts. Designed to keep play areas safe and secure while maintaining clear sightlines for supervision. Available in chain link, wire mesh, and panel systems to suit the environment.

🚧

08

Temporary & Construction Fencing

Rapid-deployment fencing for active construction sites, public events, road closures, and temporary enclosures. Keeps unauthorised persons out, protects equipment, and ensures site compliance. Available for short or long-term hire and installation across Lagos and nationwide.
